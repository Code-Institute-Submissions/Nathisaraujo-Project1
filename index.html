Nathalia

27 years old, born in Brazil, mum to Thomas, aspiring artist, learning software development

Read More! Nathalia left everything behind in Brazil to study in Ireland. She found out she was pregnant 10 days after arriving in the country and since then her life changed. Now the most important thing is her baby, Thomas, who inspired her to write about her experience being a mother. She wrote a book, a song and painted some pictures to her baby and she's more than happy to be able to share this with the world. Creativity and art is part of her life.

Ram Tãopica

56 years old, born in Brazil, song-writer, Nobel Prize Winner

Read More! Ram Tãopica spent most of his life, specially on the 70's, studying about babies and in 1996 he released the song "Capitão do Mar", his masterpiece, which won the Music Nobel Prize in 2004 when he proved that this song could make any newborn baby sleep. Some years later, he presented the song "Poo or Boo" for toddlers. Later on, we found out that the lyrics of the song is related to his life's story, which is very inspiring.
